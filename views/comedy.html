Comedy

Remember, we really grew up separately; our life experience was very different because of segregation. So I think comedy is a good space to work those things and educate everyone about the different experiences and different race groups...

-- Riaad Moosa

When we in the West think of the word Muslim, we usually associate it with terrorism, Jihad or Sharia. Disassociating these analogies is pretty tough, especially when they are continuously shown to us through the media. However, armed with humor, some talented comedians use laughter to fight these prejudices. Comedy brings together people of different backgrounds and challenges their mindset by showing how ridiculous their perceptions of "others" are. Here we showcase some comedians that we think are really influencial and has been outstanding characters.

Maysoon Zayid

http://www.campuscircle.com/film/031732.jpg

Maysoon Zayid is a Palestinian-American comedian that struggles with cerebral palsy but never lets it slow her down. As a comedian and an activist, she takes on the subjects of Islamophobia, terrorism, and the Israel-Palestine conflict. She's an actress and has co-starred several comedy shows on Comedy Central. She also co-founded the New York Arab-American Comedy Festival. She has also been featured presenting on TEDwomen. Through her work, she has been a great role model; she runs a art program for disabled and orphans in refugee camps in Palestine. She has shown that having disability should not stop someone from pursuing their passion.

Maysoon Zayid performing on TED

Meena Sameh Dimian

Meena Sameh Dimian born 12.24.1981 is an American broadcaster,writer, actor and international television personality of Egyptian descent. He is most widely known as on the #1 panel talk show in Middle East: "Zahret El Khalieg" on Abu Dhabi-1. In addition, Meena was featured on an ABC News segment for their primetime news serial "What Would You Do?" where he played a Muslim store slerk dealing with racism. The video subsequently went viral and received over 10 million views. He has been featured on ABC, Comedy Central, CNN, TLC, and BBC.

Maz Jobrani

http://www.quotessays.com/images250_/maz-jobrani-4.jpg

Maz Jobrani is an Iranian born comedian who took part in the widely popular "Axis of Evil" comedy group. He has been in many films, TV shows, other comedy tours, and has done a talk on TED. Jobrani uses comedy to fight the stereotypes and prejudices against the Middle Easterners and Muslim Americans. He is a person favorite comedian of mine and he also has graduated from UC Berkeley!
