About

This website is part of University of California Berkeley's ASAMST 128AC (Muslims in America) project. Our website pertains to the Pop Culture in the Muslim community. With the negative light that the Muslim community is seen in the recent times, pop culture emulates the true value of that Islam is. Our website shows examples of modern pop culture through the mediums of comic book, comedy and hip hop that dispells the stereotypes that the Western world perceives as the "Muslim World" to be.
