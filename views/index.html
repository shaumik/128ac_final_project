Comic

Comics are just as popular as in Arab and Muslim countries as they are in West. However, we always see Western culture and values reflected in all these comics. Comics such as "99" sought to bring Islamic teachings into the world of comics by creating heros based on the 99 virtues of Islam.

Comedy

Comedy is a powerful tool that can dispel negative connotations or stereotypes. Using humor, comedians such as Meena Sameh Dimian and Maysoon Zayid show the ridiculous the stereotypes people have against Islam. Through laughter these comedic geniuses combat Islamophobia.

About

This website is part of University of California Berkeley's ASAMST 128AC (Muslims in America) project. Our website pertains to the Pop Culture in the Muslim community. With the negative light that the Muslim community is seen in the recent times, pop culture emulates the true value of that Islam is. Our website shows examples of modern pop culture through the mediums of comic book, comedy and hip hop that dispells the stereotypes that the Western world perceives as the "Muslim World" to be.
